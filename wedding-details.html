The Reception


Both the ceremony and the reception will be held at Gallow Green, a rooftop garden and restaurant in Chelsea. A cocktail hour with hors d'oeuvres, dinner, and dancing will follow the ceremony.
If you don't turn into a pumpkin by 11pm, we'll also be holding an after-party downstairs at The Heath with reserved seating, live music, and lots of bubbly.

Attire


Gallow Green is an indoor-outdoor venue with a whimsical, rustic vibe. We suggest smart-casual garden-appropriate attire with layers. Though we'll be fully sheltered from any inclement weather, the temperature may dip at night. Ditch the heels and bring your dancing shoes–there's a set of railroad tracks and some uneven paving stones that you'll feel more comfortable navigating in flats.
