It started with a hurricane…



During the summer of 2011, Hurricane Irene shut down many parts of the city. Facing a strongly recommend evacuation, Andre packed up his jar of peanut butter and a bag of potatoes, and headed downtown to spend the weekend at Arielle’s apartment.

Arielle won Andre over with home-cooked meals and many rounds of Guitar Hero. Andre won Arielle over with a Van Morrison playlist and a promise to never Google a recipe for mashed potatoes again.

After months of covert planning, Andre surprised Arielle in September 2015 with a proposal on the High Line, a party with dear friends and family from all over the country, and many donut pub donuts.
